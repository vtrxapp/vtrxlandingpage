COMMON QUESTIONS

Still Deciding?

Do I need a credit card for the 30-day free trial?

No card required to start your trial. You won't be charged until your 30 days are up — cancel anytime before then and you pay nothing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from Account Settings whenever you like, even during the free trial. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Will this work if I've never lifted before?

Yes. VTRX is built for beginners — your plan starts from the basics and adjusts as your fitness level improves.

What happens if I miss a workout?

Streak Freeze protects your progress if you miss a day — no judgment, no starting your streak over.

Can I use VTRX at home, or do I need a gym?

Both — set your available equipment (gym, dumbbells, or bodyweight only) and your plan is built around it.
